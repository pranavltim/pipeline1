Royal Enfield Himalayan

The Royal Enfield Himalayan is a versatile adventure bike designed for both on-road and off-road riding. It features a powerful engine, rugged design, and advanced suspension for a smooth ride.

Specifications:

  • Engine: 411cc, Single Cylinder
  • Power: 24.3 HP
  • Torque: 32 Nm
  • Weight: 199 kg
  • Fuel Capacity: 15 liters
